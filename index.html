HOW SAFE CLASS WORKS

Step 1

Enter the required details below and press submit. If presented with an error message, close the web page and re-open the website.

Step 2

Your Zoom Class will open in an anti-cheat page and will detect any tab or window change. If detected, you'll be given a warn.

Step 3

The warn limit on the page is 3. If crossed, your session will be terminated and your name will be sent to your coordinator.

Step 4

Make sure that you use our platform only to attend your classes as the students using it will be visible to your coordinator.

ENTER THE DETAILS BELOW TO START

Make sure that the Meeting Number and Password are entered correctly.


FEATURES

Warnings & Session Termination

  • The total number of warnings a student is allowed is 3.
  • Warns are given on detecting a tab or a window change.
  • After crossing 3 warns, the session termination screen rolls in.
  • Not returning back within 5 minutes of a warning will result in termination too.
  • The student wouldn't be able to rejoin the class even if he reloads or goes back.
  • Click to see demo (echo3D Video)

Name Recordings & Visibility to the Coordinator

  • On crossing 3 warns, your name and email are sent to the respective coordinator.
  • The list of students are presented to the coordinator in a .csv file for ease of viewing.
  • Click to see demo (echo3D Video)

Custom Instructions for Students

  • It instructs the students on the steps to follow if they are stuck on a particular screen.
  • These instructions are meant to guide the students during a class and can be customized as per the request of the coordinator.
  • Click to see demo (echo3D Video)

A Full Screen Viewing Experience

  • Once on the anti-cheat page, students will be needed to attend the class in a Full-Screen mode.
  • The Full-Screen Mode is forced as it prevents the students from getting distracted during their classes.
  • Click to see demo (echo3D Video)

Visibility of Students using the Platform

  • The students attending the class through our platform will be visibile to the coordinator.
  • The coordinator can view such students just by clicking on the top left Apps icon of their Zoom Meeting.
  • Click to see demo (echo3D Video)

Disabling JavaScript won't work

  • This is for all the geeky students who watch too much tips & tricks videos on how to cheat.
  • Even if a student disables JavaScript for the website, they won't be able to attend the meeting.
  • Click to see demo (echo3D Video)

Camera Shots of Students during the Class

  • In an interval of every 10 minutes, the camera of the student's device will take a shot.
  • This will be used to determine whether the student is actually attending the class or not.
  • The shots of the students who were caught bunking will be shared with the coordinator in the same list in a base64 format.
  • Click to see demo (echo3D Video)
