Our Mission

Safe Class is an easy-to-use service based platform made for teachers, schools and colleges who conduct their classes via Zoom. Our goal is to stop the usage of unfair means by students during online classes which are meant to improve their academic caliber. Our platform ensures that transparency is maintained with the coordinator and they get to see which of their students tried bunking the class.

Our Vision

Our main aim while making this platform was that it shouldn't be a hassle for the teachers who are already using Zoom and who find it hard switching platforms. With our unique tab change detection and session termination technology, we aim to empower online education by securing online classes and lectures which are the fundamental criterias of imparting the much needed knowledge to a student.
